Yoga Practice is for...

Everybody

Whether you're young or old, fit or fragile, there is always a yoga practice to meet your level of ability. With regular practice, people of every shape and size can build strength and flexibility.

Everywhere

Yoga can be practiced anywhere. All you need is space for your body to move. Practicing yoga on a mat is great but so is practicing at your desk, on a chair, standing in a queue or even in your kitchen!

Anytime

Many people like to practice yoga first thing in the morning to energise and set their intention, or Sankalpa for the day ahead. Others perfer to use yoga as a wind-down choosing a restorative practice to relax and improve sleep. The joy of yoga is it's available to you all the time!

Alone, Together

Whether you practice with us online, or join us in person on a lawn, yoga is a great way to connect, both with yourself and with your welcoming yoga community.
